Intro

Hi! I'm Esteban, I'm a programmer. I studied videogame development at the Complutense university in Madrid. I've worked on various games as well as in the game industry developing accessibility validation tools for EA as an intern. I'm familiar with agile methodologies, as well as version control systems and continuous integration.
My interest for programming started when I was around 10 years old, messing with scripts other people uploaded on Roblox and it continued evolving into a career.
I love constantly trying out new hobbies, the ones I like the most are photography, reading and of course gaming. I also make time to play tabletop with my friends whenever our schedules line up.

Work

Electronic Arts | Software Engineer Intern | January 2022 - July 2022
Co-lead the development of internal tools for compliance and certification testing for games published by EA.
The tool was used by various deparments, implemented agile development techniques and continous integration to adapt to changing demands and needs and always have a working product.

Experience

  • PTSD Engine and Games | Game engine made in C++ with games made in Lua
    Made a 3D game engine with physics. Games for this engine are coded entirely in Lua based on an ECS system.
    Made two games for this engine: Glock the Clock and TaiFighter.
  • Primates Perfectos | ComJamOn2022 Game jam game
    Satirical game about creating your own monkey images and selling them on the internet for profit based on user's interests.
    Got second place in a weekend-long gameJam ComJamOn 2022
  • Dream Spy | Roblox game
    Single player story about infiltrating a person's dreams to obtain documents they hide in their subconscious.
    Made in under ten days for the Roblox developers dream jam.
  • Programa Animalista de Chillar Muy Alto | Voice controlled jam game
    You are a cat giving birth and have to get to the hospital. The problem? The cab driver doesn't know how to get there and you can't drive, so the player has to actually shout out directions to the driver while trying no to pass out.
    Got honorable mention in the game jam for most modifiers implemented, such as no text in the entire game or just having 'meows' as communication.
    Made for the Fdi JamOn 2021.
  • PCE - Programa de Convivencia Espacial | Online multiplayer party game in C++
    Have all out battles with your roommates for the TV remote while living in a space station.
    Programmed entirely in C++ with SDL and Bullet physics. Catch the trailer here.
  • Fix the ship! | Roblox jam game
    Inspired by space team, you and your friends have to collaborate to fix a sinking ship while under cannon fire by shouting and receiving instructions. Made completely solo for the Infinite Canvas 'two is one, one is none' game jam in under three days of work.
